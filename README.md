### BACKEND OF A SMALL ECOMMERCE
-> It implements a backend where you can:
  1. Create a new account
  2. Login in with you account with JWT
  3. See all products (and by Sellers or name)
  4. Create your shopping cart
  5. Buy what you have in your shopping cart (no real money, obviously)
  6. See your orders because you will have as many orders as Sellers where in the products of your shopping cart

###### You can see all MVC in Develop Branch
